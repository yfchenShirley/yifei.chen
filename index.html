About Me

My Work

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Groningen. I work in the Bernoulli Institute for Mathematics, Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence with Prof. dr. L.R.B. Schomaker and Dr. M.A. Wiering.

My research topic includes the two main questions: (a) How to reduce the large number of required experiences of DRL for obtaining a good controller? (b) How to effectively transfer knowledge gained from one task to quickly solve new tasks? I will mainly focus on HRL and transfer learning.
